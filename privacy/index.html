Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Amon Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates an internal dashboard tool for managing and analyzing TikTok account performance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect a TikTok account to Amon Studio, we collect the following information via the TikTok API:

  • TikTok account username and display name
  • Follower count, following count, and likes count
  • Video list and associated metrics (views, likes, comments)
  • Profile information (avatar, bio)

We also collect your email address for authentication purposes when you log into the dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Display account performance metrics within the Amon Studio dashboard
  • Track historical performance trends over time
  • Enable content publishing to connected TikTok accounts

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Account data and performance snapshots are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Access tokens provided by TikTok are stored locally in your browser and are used solely to make authorized API requests on your behalf.

4. TikTok API Usage

Amon Studio uses the TikTok API in accordance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service. We only request permissions necessary for the features described above. You may revoke access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings.

5. Data Retention

Performance snapshots are retained for up to 12 months to enable historical trend analysis. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure database access controls.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.
